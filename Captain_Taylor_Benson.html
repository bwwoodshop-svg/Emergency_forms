📌 How to find this form again
iPhone: Tap Share (□↑) → "Add to Home Screen" — it will appear as an app icon on your home screen.
Android: Tap ⋮ menu → "Add to Home Screen" — same result.
Map tiles are saved in your browser's cache and stay available offline as long as you open the form from the same browser (or home screen icon). Do not clear browser data before an emergency.
⚠️ Important
Do NOT refresh the page during an emergency — refreshing clears your map view and may lose unsaved data if you are offline.
Send Text on iPhone: The "Send Text" button does not work from the home screen icon (Web App mode). To send texts, open the form in Safari instead. The home screen icon is fine for everything else.
